Help

How to submit data:

  • Press the “submit” button on the home page or go to the “Submit a measurement” screen from the main menu.
  • Fill out the fields on the submit page
  • Station ID Number: If there’s a descriptive plaque from the CrowdHydrology plaque near the post, enter the ID from that plaque. Otherwise, leave this field blank.
  • Water Level: Enter the water level you see on the measuring post here, in feet.
  • Clarity: Pick the clarity level that best matches the conditions near the shore of your body of water.
  • Picture of Water Level: Select “choose file” to take a picture of the measuring post and send it in. If you can zoom in enough to read the numbers on the post, that’s helpful.
  • Press “submit”.

How to view the map of measuring stations:

  • From the main menu, select “view”. This will show you a map the CrowdHydrology measuring stations.
  • To check the data of a particular station, press the icon that marks its location. A bubble will pop up, giving the station number, water body, and a link that says “view data”. Press that link to view the data.
  • Data you enter is automatically added to the database, and will show up when you the view data shortly afterward.
